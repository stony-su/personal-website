Me and my friends went to the biggest hackathon in Toronto! The hackathon was at Shopify headquaters, and they went all out with the snacks, activies, and prizes! The whole experiance was a blast (more like a party really) and our whole team had a lot of fun!

As for our project, we created a zombie attack drone, who couuld:

  • Spray water (replace with acid) on the zombies
  • Launch a balista bolt

To create our project, we used:

  • Python
  • Rasberry Pi
  • Ardio

Things I learned from this Hackathon:

  • Make Schueles! Time mangement is super important, otherwise your sleep schules ifs screwed big time ( we had to pull two all-nights to debug our code ;-; )
  • Don't bite off more than you can chew! We challenaged ourselfs so much stuff that the final product's design looked ass (litterally just two plates holding all the hardware in)
  • Have Fun! There were so many fun activies but we didn't go to any of them *cries*

Huge shout out to the entire Apocalypse Hacks team, espicelly Greg who goes to my school!
